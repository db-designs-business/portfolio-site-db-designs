About me

My Story

Hey, my name is Dominic Brecht. I’m a User Interface and Corporate Designer always striving to create the perfect solution for the customer and present them in the best way either digital or print. In the past I gathered experience in print design & sign making and then I gained further education in User Interface Design. I enjoy working on new and challenging projects as I can gather new insights and improve my skills.

As I have some business on the side I know exactly what the customer needs and I’m capable of solving their problems to the roots. I am a team player, creative, and versatile in meeting project needs while building strong connections with colleagues and clients alike. Let me know how I can help add value to your design team!
